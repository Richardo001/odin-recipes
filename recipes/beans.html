Beans Porridge

Prepared Beans

This is a fully prepared beans ready to be devoured by me, lol. I love this food

Ingredients

  • Groundnut Oil
  • Pepper
  • Tomatoes
  • Meat
  • Vegetables
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Put Oil
  2. Fry it
  3. Put petter
  4. Put Meat
  5. Put Vegetables
  6. Put Onions
  7. mix together
  8. Allow to cook for 15 minutes
  9. Ready to serve!
